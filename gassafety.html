Gas Safety Certificate

Stop that Leak Plumbing Solutions are the number one choice for many landlords and letting agents across Grimsby and North East Lincolnshire. We provide servicing and maintenance for plumbing and central heating systems in single properties as well as across entire property portfolios.

As a landlord, you are required by law to have all gas appliances checked every 12 months and to hold a Gas Safety Certificate (CP12) to ensure gas fittings and appliances are safe to use. These checks must be carried out by a registered Gas Safe engineer.We can provide landlords with a gas safety certificate to help them meet their statutory and legal requirements.

And they don’t have to remember the renewal date. Our management system takes all that hassle away, ensuring the Landlord’s Gas Safety Certificates are renewed and updated regularly.
