Plumbing & Heating

If someone is looking for boiler repairs in Grimsby, heating engineers in Grimsby, boiler installation or boiler servicing, he or she can always opt to search for their chosen engineer via the internet. However there is no need Stop that Leak Plumbing Solutions can provide the solution. Our engineers have worked on some extremely old and out dated boilers and heating systems and due to the amount of sheer determination and ability that they have they have managed to restore either the boiler or heating system into a working order.

Has your boiler given up the ghost?  Are you wearing too many jumpers? Let the experienced team here at Stop that Leak Plumbing Solutions find out what’s wrong.  We will then service and repair your central heating system for you, so that you can start walking around inside again without your fleece on!   Alternatively, if you want a new boiler installed or want to upgrade to an energy efficient boiler, we’ll give you all the advice you need to help you make your choice.

Safety and efficiency are at the heart of everything we do, so we always make sure any new gas central heating system we supply and install is suitable for your property.  Powerful enough to heat it, yet efficient, reliable and cost effective at the same time. All of our heating engineers in Grimsby are time-served and Gas Safe Registered.  That means you can rest assured that they are fully qualified to carry out any necessary work safely.  We are also approved suppliers for a number of quality manufactures, including Veissman Boilers, Worcester Bosch and Ideal Logic.

If you find yourself in hot water, because there’s a serious lack of it in your home, give us a call.  Our time-served engineers will check your heating system, immersion heater and tank (if you have one) so you can either get back to washing up, or having a relaxing shower or bath, in no time at all. Or if you find you need a new boiler, immersion heater, water cylinder or tank, just let us know.  We’ll find the perfect solution for your home and install it for you.
